Canceling Sessions

Generally, at the end of a session, I will attempt to plan the next one. The customer has no obligation to book another session. You can back out from my services whenever you want to, with no penalty or extra costs.

Additionally, if you book a session, but something comes up and the pupil is unable to attend, you can cancel the session by getting in touch. I ask only that you try and notify me at least 24 hours before the planned session.
